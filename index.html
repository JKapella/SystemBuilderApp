System Builder V 1.0

A work in progress procedural star system generator. Hit the 'Generate' button below to create your system. Currently the generator will create G-type main-sequence stars, and a planetary system of between 1 and 10 planets.

Coming next: Procedural star names and correct planet names, better planet distribution, more stellar and planetary characteristics, refinement to generate the system and planets from a single pre-stellar cloud, moons, other star types, comets and asteroids, binaries and multiple star systems, exotic stars.
